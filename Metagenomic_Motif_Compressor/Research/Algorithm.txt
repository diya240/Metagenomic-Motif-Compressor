#Metagenomic Motif Compressor Algorithm

1. Read the DNA sequence from the input file.
2. Scan the sequence to identify repeated motifs.
3. Count motif occurrences.
4. Select motifs appearing more than once.
5. Create a dictionary of motifs.
6. Assign reference identifiers to each motif.
7. Replace repeated motifs with references.
8. Generate compressed DNA output.
9. Store dictionary and compressed sequence.
10. Enable reconstruction of original DNA.
